# Ginger Tea  
## Ingredients  
- 1 cup water  
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger  
- 1 teaspoon honey  
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice  
## Instructions  
- Boil water in a pot.  
- Add grated ginger and simmer for 5 minutes.  
- Strain the tea into a cup.  
- Stir in honey and lemon juice.  
- Serve hot.
