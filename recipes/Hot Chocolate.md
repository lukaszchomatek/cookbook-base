# Hot Chocolate  

## Ingredients  
- 2 cups milk  
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder  
- 1 tablespoon sugar  
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract  

## Instructions  
1. Heat milk in a saucepan over medium heat until warm.  
2. Whisk in cocoa powder and sugar until fully dissolved.  
3. Continue heating until mixture is hot but not boiling.  
4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.  
5. Serve in a mug.
